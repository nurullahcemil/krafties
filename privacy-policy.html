Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Krafties ("we," "our," or "us") handles information in connection with our iPhone applications and website (collectively, the "Services").

Our Privacy Commitment

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our apps are designed with privacy in mind:

  • No Sign-Up Required: None of our apps require you to create an account or sign up.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.
  • Local Storage Only: All data created or used within our apps is stored locally on your device only.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools that would collect or share your data.

Information We DO NOT Collect

Unlike many other apps, we do not collect:

  • Personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • User browsing history
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Usage patterns for marketing purposes

Device Storage

Our apps may store the following types of information locally on your device:

  • App preferences and settings
  • Content you create within the app
  • Cached data to improve app performance

This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Apple App Store

When you download our apps through the Apple App Store, Apple may collect certain information as described in their Privacy Policy. This collection is governed by Apple's terms and privacy practices.

Website Usage

When you visit our website:

  • We do not use cookies for tracking purposes
  • We do not collect personal information through the website
  • Any contact form submissions are processed through Formspree and are subject to their privacy policy

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through our website or by updating the app with a notice.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your personal data, there is no personal information for us to provide, correct, or delete. However, you may:

  • Delete the app from your device at any time
  • Clear local data by deleting and reinstalling the app
  • Contact us with any questions about this policy
